BINARY TREE

	Full
		Leaves have no children
		Nodes that do have children, have 2 children
	Complete
		Like binary heaps
		Fill out top to bottom, left to right
	Perfect
		Both complete and FULL
		All leaves are on the same level

	Traversals

		N = Node, L = Left, R = Right
		Preorder
			N L R
		Inorder
			L N R
		Postorder
			L R N
